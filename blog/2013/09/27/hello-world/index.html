Let the Adventure Begin!

Sep 27th, 2013

First week of Flatiron school is complete and what a week it has been! From flying cross country and putting 7 years of my life in storage on September 21st, to starting school on September 23rd. I worked in the Boeing company for seven years; the first five years I was on the design team for the cabin interior of the 787. My proudest professional experience was from building something from nothing. Back in 2006 the 787 Dreamliner was just a paper airplane it existed in powerpoint presentations and excel spreadsheet.

I started working in the Boeing company on July 21st 2013 with the sole intend of building an amazing airplane! For the next five and a half years it was an amazing experience I worked with some of the best engineers in the world, learning how to build an airplane, travelled all over the world and even had time to run a marathon or two. After we built the airplane worked slowed down a lot, I wasn’t building anything new and for going to work became boring.

For the next 12 weeks I will be immersed in code as I will try to build amazing things on web for all to see. Come follow me on this journey.
